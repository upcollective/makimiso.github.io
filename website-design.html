Website Design

Custom, user-friendly websites optimized for engagement, conversion, and accessibility, specifically designed for nonprofits and educational institutions.

Websites That Drive Your Mission Forward

Your website is often the first impression potential supporters, clients, or beneficiaries have of your organization. We design and develop websites that not only look professional but also effectively communicate your mission and drive action.

Our approach combines beautiful design with strategic thinking, ensuring your website serves as a powerful tool for engagement, fundraising, and impact communication.

What We Deliver

  • Responsive design that works perfectly on all devices
  • User-friendly content management system (CMS)
  • Accessibility compliance (WCAG 2.1 AA standards)
  • Search engine optimization (SEO) foundation
  • Fast loading speeds and performance optimization
  • Integration with donation platforms and CRM systems
  • Volunteer sign-up and event registration forms
  • Newsletter signup and email marketing integration
  • Social media integration
  • Analytics and tracking setup

Our Process

1. Discovery & Strategy

We start by understanding your organization, goals, and target audience. We analyze your current digital presence and identify opportunities for improvement.

2. Design & Prototyping

Our team creates wireframes and visual designs that reflect your brand and mission. We ensure the design supports your key user journeys and conversion goals.

3. Development & Testing

We build your website using modern, secure technologies. Every page is tested across devices and browsers to ensure optimal performance.

4. Launch & Training

We handle the technical launch and provide training so your team can easily manage content updates, events, and ongoing communications.

Why Choose Makimiso for Your Website

We understand the unique challenges that nonprofits and educational institutions face online. Our websites are designed to:

  • Build Trust: Professional design and clear messaging that establishes credibility with visitors
  • Drive Action: Strategic placement of donation buttons, volunteer forms, and contact information
  • Tell Your Story: Compelling visual storytelling that showcases your impact and mission
  • Improve Accessibility: Inclusive design that serves all users, regardless of ability
  • Optimize for Search: SEO best practices to help people find your organization online
  • Integrate Seamlessly: Connect with your existing tools and platforms

Frequently Asked Questions

How long does it take to build a website?

Most websites take 6-12 weeks from start to finish, depending on complexity and content requirements. We'll provide a detailed timeline during our initial consultation.

Will I be able to update the website myself?

Absolutely! We build websites with user-friendly content management systems and provide training so you can easily update content, add events, and manage your site.

Do you provide ongoing support?

Yes, we offer maintenance packages to keep your website secure, updated, and performing optimally. We also provide training and documentation for your team.

How do you ensure accessibility?

We follow WCAG 2.1 AA guidelines and test our websites with screen readers and other assistive technologies to ensure they're accessible to all users.

Can you integrate with our existing tools?

Yes! We can integrate your website with most CRM systems, donation platforms, email marketing tools, and other software your organization uses.

What about mobile devices?

All our websites are designed mobile-first and fully responsive, ensuring they work perfectly on smartphones, tablets, and desktop computers.
